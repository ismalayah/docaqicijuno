Aegislash ex (Paradox Rift 135)

Publish date: 2024-08-16

From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.

Aegislash (Japanese: ギルガルドex Gillgard ex) is a Metal-type Stage 2 Pokémon ex card. It is part of the Paradox Rift expansion.

Card text

Peerless Edge
ピアレスエッジ		70×
This attack does 70 damage for each Prize card you have taken.
Double-Edged Slash
もろはぎり		220
This Pokémon also does 30 damage to itself.

Release information

This card was included as both a Regular card and a Full Art ultra rare card in the Paradox Rift expansion, first released in the Japanese Future Flash expansion. The Regular print features artwork by toriyufu, while the Full Art ultra rare print features artwork by Satoshi Shirai.

Trivia

Origin

This card features Aegislash in its Blade Forme. Double-Edged Slash is an attack that first appeared on Bisharp from Scarlet & Violet.
