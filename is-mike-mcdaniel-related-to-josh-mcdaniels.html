Is Mike McDaniel related to Josh McDaniels?

Publish date: 2024-08-01

Despite the fact that they share the same surname and are both well-known NFL coaches, Mike McDaniel and Josh McDaniels are not related.

This is a misconception that is prevalent among many football fans, but it is simply not true.

Mike McDaniel is the current head coach of the Miami Dolphins. He previously served as the offensive coordinator for the San Francisco 49ers, and has held various assistant coaching positions with the Denver Broncos, Houston Texans, Washington Redskins, Cleveland Browns, and Atlanta Falcons.

Josh McDaniels is the current head coach of the Las Vegas Raiders. He is widely regarded as one of the best offensive coordinators in NFL history, and has won six Super Bowls with the New England Patriots.

While Mike and Josh McDaniels are not related, they do have some things in common. Both men are considered to be bright offensive minds, and both have worked under some of the most successful coaches in the NFL.

Mike McDaniel is a disciple of Kyle Shanahan, while Josh McDaniels is a protege of Bill Belichick.

It is also worth noting that Mike and Josh McDaniels have crossed paths several times during their careers. In 2009, Mike McDaniel was a coaching intern for the Denver Broncos, while Josh McDaniels was the head coach.

In 2017 and 2020, Mike and Josh McDaniels faced off each other as assistant coaches in the Super Bowl.

Despite their shared last name, lack of familial relation, and similar careers, Mike and Josh McDaniels are two very different people.

Mike McDaniel is known for his laid-back personality and his innovative offensive schemes.

Josh McDaniels is known for his demanding coaching style and his focus on fundamentals.
