Scorching Charcoal
対熱のもくたん Scorching Charcoal

Illus. Toyste Beach
(This card cannot be used at official tournaments.)

Scorching Charcoal (Japanese: 対熱のもくたん Scorching Charcoal) is a Pokémon Tool card. It is part of the Pokémon Trading Card Game Classic Collection.

Card text

The attacks used by the Charizard this card is attached to do 100 more damage to your opponent's Active Pokémon (before applying Weakness and Resistance).
You may attach any number of Pokémon Tools to your Pokémon during your turn. You may attach only 1 Pokémon Tool to each Pokémon, and it stays attached.
