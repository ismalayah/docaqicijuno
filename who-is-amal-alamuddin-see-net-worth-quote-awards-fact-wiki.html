Amal Ramzi Alamuddin Wiki Biography

Amal Ramzi Alamuddin was born on 3 February 1978, in Beirut Lebanon, somewhat unusually into a Druze and Muslim family. Recently Amal became even more recognized as she married the famous actor, George Clooney. However, Alamuddin is an acclaimed lawyer and has earned the acclaim of others.
